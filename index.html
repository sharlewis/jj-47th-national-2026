Give the Gift of Life

As we gather in Baltimore, we invite the whole Jack and Jill family to join a powerful act of service — donating blood with the American Red Cross.

1

One unit of donated blood can save up to three lives.

2

In the United States, the need disproportionately affects people of African descent.

3

Sickle Cell Disease affects 1 in every 365 Black or African American births, and 90% of people living with it in the U.S. are of African descent.

4

Every two seconds, someone in this country needs a blood transfusion.

5

Blood from Black donors is often the best match for patients with Sickle Cell Disease — making every donation from our community especially critical.
